Overview

What's covered, and what isn't

Refund eligibility depends on project stage and service type. Here's the honest breakdown before you commit to anything.

Refundable
  • ✓Full refund if work hasn't started within 14 days of payment.
  • ✓Partial refund if only discovery or strategy phase is complete.
  • ✓Retainer refund for any unused, unbilled month.
  • ✓Full refund if Zynocta misses the agreed first-draft deadline by 10+ days.
  • ✓Duplicate or accidental payments, refunded in full, no questions asked.
Non-refundable
  • ✕Final deliverables that have been approved and handed over.
  • ✕Third-party costs already spent (domains, stock assets, ad spend).
  • ✕Custom or rush projects marked "final sale" at checkout.
  • ✕Change-of-mind requests made after milestone 2 sign-off.
  • ✕Work delayed due to client-side non-response beyond 30 days.

Special Cases

Exceptions worth knowing

A handful of situations fall outside the standard rule. Here's how each one is handled.

Zynocta misses a deadline+
If we miss an agreed milestone deadline by more than 10 calendar days without prior notice, you can request a full refund for any unbilled or incomplete portion of the project — regardless of the standard 14-day window.
You cancel mid-retainer+
Monthly retainers are billed in advance. Cancelling before the next billing cycle gets you a full refund of that unused month; work already delivered in the current month is not refunded.
Chargebacks and disputes+
We ask that you contact us before filing a bank chargeback. Most disputes are resolved faster through a direct refund request, and an open chargeback will pause any active project work until resolved.
Rush and custom orders+
Projects explicitly marked "final sale" at checkout — typically rush turnarounds under 72 hours — are non-refundable once work begins, since resources are allocated immediately upon payment.
FAQ

Common questions

Still unsure about something? These are the questions our support team hears most often.

Do I need a reason to request a refund?+
Not a detailed one — a short explanation helps us process faster, but eligibility is based on project stage and timing, not on justifying your decision.
Can I get store credit instead of cash back?+
Yes. On request, we can issue eligible refunds as account credit with an added 10% bonus, valid toward any future Zynocta service for 12 months.
What if I paid through a third-party platform?+
Refunds are always issued back through the same platform or processor you paid with. Processing times may be slightly longer depending on that platform's own policies.
Does a refund cancel my project entirely?+
Yes, a processed refund closes out the associated project or billing cycle. If you'd rather adjust scope instead of cancelling, mention that in your request — we're usually happy to find a middle ground.
