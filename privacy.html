01 · Data Collection

Information we collect

We only collect what's needed to deliver your project well and support you afterward — nothing decorative.

Account & contact info

Name, email, phone, billing address — provided when you sign up or request a quote.

Project & brand assets

Files, briefs, and content you upload for us to design, build, or market with.

Usage & device data

Browser type, pages visited, and rough location — collected automatically for site analytics.

02 · Purpose

How we use it

Every use of your data ties back to one of these purposes — we don't repurpose it beyond this list.

deliver

To scope, build, and hand off the project or service you've hired us for.

support

To respond to questions, troubleshoot issues, and process refund or billing requests.

improve

To understand how our site and services are used so we can fix friction points.

notify

To send project updates, invoices, and — only if you opt in — occasional product news.

protect

To detect fraud, secure accounts, and meet our legal and tax obligations.

05 · Safeguards

Retention & security

How long we keep things, and what protects them while we do.

Encrypted storage, access limited to staff on a need-to-know basis, and regular third-party audits of our infrastructure.
